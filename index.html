Measurement settings

Z is vertical when the phone lies flat on the floor — leave it on Z for a standard heel-drop. High-pass ≥ 1 Hz removes slow drift before peak-picking (matches the iDynamics recommendation).

Heel-drop procedure

  1. Place the phone flat on the floor at the test point and stand still so the trace shows only noise.
  2. Press record. Wait for the “rest detected” state.
  3. Lift both heels so you stand on your toes.
  4. Bring both heels down forcefully onto the floor — one clean impact.
  5. Stand still and let the floor ring down for the full duration.
  6. Recording auto-stops; review the analysis, then save or export.
